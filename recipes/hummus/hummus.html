This recipe is from Chefkoch and it shows you how to make Merceiles Hummus turkish style

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of chickpeas(400g)
  • 2 Garlic clove(s), crushed in a mortar with a little salt
  • 2 teaspoon(s) olive oil
  • 3 teaspoon(s) lemon juice(fresh pressed)
  • 4 teaspoon(s) sesame paste
  • 1 teaspoon(s) salt
  • 1 teaspoon(s) Cumin
  • 1 teaspoon(s) Sweet paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon(s) Paprika powder, rosy hot

Instructions

  1. Drain chickpeas (or use equivalent amount of self-cooked chickpeas), reserve some for garnish if desired, and collect the draining liquid.
  2. Blend all remaining ingredients with an immersion blender. If the mixture is too thick, add some of the collected chickpea liquid until it reaches the approximate consistency of mashed potatoes.
  3. Note: Adjust tahini and other spices to taste; experiment and season to preference.
  4. Tips: For dried chickpeas, soak overnight and cook for about 45 minutes in unsalted water. Consider the salt content if using canned chickpeas. Cooked chickpeas can be frozen for later use in hummus or soups.
