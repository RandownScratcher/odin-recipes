Pancake

This recipe is from Chefkoch and it shows you how to make the ultimate juicy pancake!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon(s) sugar
  • 400ml milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 200g flour
  • 1 package backing powder
  • oil for baking

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs and sugar until creamy, then mix with milk.
  2. Add salt, flour, and baking powder, stir into a smooth batter.
  3. Let the batter rest for a while to allow the flour to swell.
  4. Pour 1-2 large ladles of batter into a preheated, non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. After about 2 minutes, small bubbles will form; flip the pancake and cook until golden brown on the other side.
